Season tickets

Season tickets cost €70 per person (€50 for students, as well as cast & crew).
Please note: cast and crew of competing groups have free access to the other performances on the of their own performance only.

Although there is no separate link to order season tickets, you can order them by clicking on any of the days below and selecting the season ticket option, which will get you a ticket for all four nights.

Day tickets

Tickets for individual nights cost €20 per person (€15 for students only)

Here you can book tickets for:

  • Saturday, 18 May
  • Sunday, 19 May
  • Monday, 20 May
